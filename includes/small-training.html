Small Diameter Operator Qualification

Covers sizes ranging from ½" CTS - 6" DIPS, including socket fusion and hot tap.

The Small Diameter Operator Qualification session prepares students who work in small diameter applications from ½" CTS to 6" DIPS (16mm to 180mm) to understand the theory of fusion and properly fuse small diameter pipes and fittings. Participants go hands-on with our smallest machines, the Mini-Mc® and 1LC, and learn saddle fusion techniques on the Sidewinder®. The course also covers some of McElroy’s most popular machines, such as the Pit Bull® 14, 2LC and Pit Bull® 26.

This 3 day course covers:

  • Fusion Theory
  • Proper Fusion Techniques
  • Sidewall Fusion
  • Application Tips
  • Equipment Design
  • Troubleshooting Resources
  • DOT Requirements (PHMSA)
  • Preventative Maintenance
  • ASTM Standards
  • Mini-Mc®
  • 1LC
  • Socket Fusion
  • Sidewinder®
  • 2LC
  • 2CU
  • Pit Bull® 14
  • Pit Bull® 26
